TRIDENT PRO X

Our Trident Pro X Series is a powerful whole house fan that lives up to its name. Consisting of several models, this lineup can produce airflow ranging from 1,500 CFM all the way up to 7,000. The Trident Pro X has an energy-efficient Permanent Split Capacitor Motor (PSC). This Permanent Split Capacitor Motor uses sealed ball bearings to increase durability and longevity. This Whole House Fan is sold by local authorized dealers and installers.

  • Permanent Split Capacitor Motor (PSC)
  • 115 watts to 1,147 watts depending on model

TRADITIONAL ATTIC FANS

Traditional Attic Fans are what most homeowners have in their homes when it comes to attic fans. These fans push a lot of air, are powerful, and reliable. These fans often come with a thermostat that can be set to when you want your attic fan to run and can push air at up to 2,000 CFM. These attic fans are typically less expensive and perfect for anyone who wants a cheap alternative to their high A/C costs and reliable production to keep their attic and home cool.

  • Plug-and-play (no wiring)
  • Energy-Efficient two-speed capable PSC Motor
  • Hi: 1,945 CFM / Lo: 1,495 CFM
